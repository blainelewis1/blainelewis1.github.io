About me

I'm 22 and I was born in Edmonton, Canada. I love to travel, rock climb and be in a constant state of change. Whether it's a fast paced work environment or learning a new hobby I never stop.

When I was 12 years old my Uncle's girlfriend gave me a copy of Adobe Flash MX and I started making my first game. From there I took off learning a variety of languages and had my first internship in grade 10. I've worked for large companies like Microsoft as a program manager intern, wrote websites for friends and even worked in IT departments on enterprise solutions. I don't know what I want to work with but right now I spend my free time writing Javascript because it's so accessible and I can show my friends what I'm working on.

Don't be scared to contact me, I'd love to hear from you!
